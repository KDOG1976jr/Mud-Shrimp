Reproduction occurs through external fertilization, with females carrying eggs on their back legs, until they hatch. Male mud shrimp transfer a spermatophore to specialized receptacle on the females abdomen. The femal waves the eggs around on her legs to give them oxygen and to clean them regulary, until they are born.
